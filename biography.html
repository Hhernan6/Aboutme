Who am I?

Background

I was born in Michoacan, Mexico in March 4, 1998. I came to the United States when I was just five years old. I do not remember much from Mexico. I also do not remember when I came over here. I really hate the fact that I can not remember much about these two things. I have 5 brothers and one sister and I am the youngest. My sibilings had a hard time adapting to the new life we had. I do remember my first day of kindergarden and how I cried. It was one of the scariest days of my life.
